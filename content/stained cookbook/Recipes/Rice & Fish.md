## Ingredients
 - [] 200g rice
 - [] 1 cucumber
 - [] 4 tomatoes
 - [] 1 410g can of mixed bean salad
 - [] 1 tin of mackerel
 - [] 1 egg
## Instructions
 1. Prepare vegetables, whilst hard boiling the egg.
 2. Combine everything in two separate bowls as shown in the image above.
 3. Mix!
## Note(s)/Tip(s)
*Two servings, one vegetarian, one pescatarian*
